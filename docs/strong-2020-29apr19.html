BaBar $\sigma(e^+e^- \rightarrow \pi^+\pi^- (\gamma))$¶

29 Apr 2019 - Julia version¶

The latest BaBar measurements are published in two papers, a PRL and a later PRD containing more detailed information. Both papers report the cross-section information in the supplemental material, in ASCII files that are identical.

  • B. Aubert et al. [BaBar Collaboration], Phys. Rev. Lett. 103 (2009) 231801, inspirehep,
    "Precise measurement of the e+ e- ---> pi+ pi- (gamma) cross section with the Initial State Radiation method at BABAR"

  • J. P. Lees et al. [BaBar Collaboration], Phys. Rev. D 86 (2012) 032013, inspirehep,
    "Precise Measurement of the e+e+()e+e+() Cross Section with the Initial-State Radiation Method at BABAR"

    • data

The data report the "bare cross section including FSR" in nb, and in detail:

  • the cross-section and its total undertainty in variable-width bins of energy
  • the per-mil relative systematic uncertainty (per energy bin, 100% correlated on all bins)
  • the statistical correlation between any two bins of cross-section

In the following the data are used to show a few plots.

##--- dictionary to convert Energy to Dataframe index
df_E_to_index = Dict(df[i, :E] => i for i = 1:nrow(df))
##--- function to get covariance as function of two energies
function stat_cov(E_x, E_y)
  row = df_E_to_index[E_x]
  col = df_E_to_index[E_y]
  n_row = nrow(df)
  df_stat[row + (col-1)*n_row, :cov] 
end

##--- range of covariance with extra 20% margin
cov_clims = @df df_stat identity(
  (
    minimum(:cov)-0.2*(maximum(:cov)-minimum(:cov)),
    maximum(:cov)+0.2*(maximum(:cov)-minimum(:cov))

)

##
## plot systematic covariance
##

##--- dictionary to convert Energy to Dataframe index
df_syst_E_to_index = Dict(df_syst[i, :E] => i for i = 1:nrow(df_syst))
##--- function to get covariance as function of two energies
function syst_cov(E_x, E_y)
  row = df_syst_E_to_index[E_x]
  col = df_syst_E_to_index[E_y]
  df_syst[row, :unc] * df_syst[col, :unc]
end

##--- compute range of covariance with extra 20% margin
rc = [syst_cov(df_syst[i, :E], df_syst[j, :E]) for i = 1:nrow(df_syst), j = 1:nrow(df_syst)]
cov_clims = (
  minimum(rc)-0.2*(maximum(rc)-minimum(rc)),
  maximum(rc)+0.2*(maximum(rc)-minimum(rc))
)
